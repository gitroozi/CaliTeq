## Progress Tracking

### Performance Metrics
- Rep maxes for each exercise
- Time-based holds (planks, L-sits)
- Skill achievements
- Volume progression
- Training frequency and adherence

### Body Metrics
- Weight tracking with trend lines
- Waist circumference
- Progress photos (front, side, back)
- Body fat percentage (optional)

### Analytics Dashboard
- Training overview
- Strength progression charts
- Body composition trends
- Recovery metrics

### Achievements & Gamification
- Milestone badges
- Strength level classifications
- Social sharing options
